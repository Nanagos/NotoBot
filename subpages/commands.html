NotoBot Commands

Command list

command description
2bplayer [player] Get information about a player that played on 2b2t
2bstatus Get status information about 2b2t
coords Shows the location of the bot
fact Get a random useless fact
favword [player] Get the top used words of a player
firstwords [username] get the firstwords of somebody
health Shows the health of the bot
help Get a list of commands or get information about a command
info Get more information about NotoBot
ip [name] Get the ip of somebody
ipinfo [ip] Get information about an ip adress
iq [name] Get the iq of somebody
kill kill the bot
lastmsg [player] Get the last words of a player
namemc [player] Get the namemc page of a player
ping Get your ping / the ping of somebody else
pings Get the best and the worst ping
players Get a list of all players
playtime [username] Get the playtime of a player
queue Get the queue length of 2b2t
quote [player] Get a random quote of a player
reddit [subreddit] Get the top post of a subreddit
say [text] force the bot to say something
seen [player] Get the last time when NotoBot has seen somebody
server [ip] Get information about a minecraft server
top [msgs/playtime] Get the a top list of players
urban [term] Search a query in the urban dictionary
whisper [target] [text] whisper to another player
wiki [query] Search a query in wikipedia new
word [player] [word] Get how often a player said a word
ytchannel [channel-name] Find a youtube channel
ytvideo [query] Search a video on youtube

Notes

The prefix is ?

You can use ?help to get a list of commands

The list is not always up to date
