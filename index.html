What's NotoBot?

NotoBot is a Minecraft bot, that is running on various cracked anarchy servers. There are 25 commands and almost all commands are usable in the Discord chat.

The bot is in active development, you can bring up your suggestion and bugs will be fixed. If you have any questions, just join my Discord.
